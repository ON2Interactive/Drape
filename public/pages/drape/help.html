Getting Started

New to Drape? Create your account, then upload your profile image, add items to your collection, and start building looks in the workspace.

  • Sign up and open the Drape workspace.
  • Upload a profile image and complete onboarding.
  • Add wardrobe pieces into your collection panel.
  • Start creating looks from the pieces you actually own.

Building Your Collection

Your collection is the foundation of the Drape workflow. The stronger your collection, the better your try-ons, stylist suggestions, and weekly plans become.

  • Upload items into your collection panel.
  • Select one or more pieces to use in a look.
  • Keep your collection current so Stylist recommendations stay accurate.
  • Use real collection items to drive try-ons, prompts, and planning.

Creating Looks

Drape gives you multiple ways to create outfits depending on how you want to work. You can use Intelligent Try On, Drape Prompt, Drape Remix, or ask the Stylist to guide the process.

  • Use Intelligent Try On to see selected items on your profile.
  • Use Drape Prompt to generate a look from selected collection pieces and instructions.
  • Use Drape Remix to change the background or scene.
  • Save the best generated looks to favourites.

Using Drape Stylist

Drape Stylist is a live assistant that can review your collection, respond to spoken requests, recommend combinations, and generate an outfit based on what you ask for.

  • Open Stylist from the left menu.
  • Use the mic to start a live session.
  • Ask for looks based on your collection and occasion.
  • Say when you are done and let Stylist generate the outfit.

Drape Plan

Drape Plan helps you turn your collection into a weekly outfit schedule. It can generate looks across the week so you can plan ahead and reduce repetition.

  • Open Plan from the workspace.
  • Generate a weekly set of looks from your collection.
  • Review each day and regenerate when needed.
  • Keep your weekly plan saved so it stays available after refresh.

Video, Favourites, and Sharing

Generated looks can move beyond still images. Drape supports video preview, favourites, downloads, and sharing so you can keep the looks that matter.

  • Generate a video preview from a look.
  • Save images and videos to favourites.
  • Download generated assets directly from the workspace.
  • Share standout looks when you are ready.

Troubleshooting

If something does not look right, most workflow issues can be resolved quickly by refreshing the browser, checking your saved collection items, or trying the action again from the workspace.

  • Refresh the browser if a common page or workspace view looks stale.
  • Make sure the items you want are actually in your collection before generating.
  • If a model action fails, try the action again before changing your setup.
  • Use the Contact page if you need direct support.
